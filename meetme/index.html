Core Tenets

Along the way, I have identified key traits that lead engineers, teams and companies to success.

DevOps

A happy engineer is a productive engineer. Protect your enthusiasm with incremental improvements to your workflow. Automation and telemetry improve agility and lead to better software. Keep current.

Customer Focus

Eye on the prize. Engineering is an art with resource scarcity. Time, money and your customer should always factor into your decisions. Let your engineers talk to the customer; don't play games of telephone.

Communication

SDLC and Customer Focus are your Yin and Yang; communication is how you keep the balance. Be sincere. Don't hide that awesome CI tool from Product. Likewise, don't celebrate the latest revenue report without your engineers!
